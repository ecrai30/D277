Auburn: The Loveliest Village on the Plains

Auburn Facts:

  • Incorporated on February 2, 1839
  • Located in eastern Alabama.
  • Population of 78,564
  • Average household income level is $54,700
  • Urban

Auburn is home to the Auburn Univeristy Tigers. The mascot is Aubie the Tiger.
