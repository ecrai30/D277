Alabama: The Beautiful

Alabama is a southeastern U.S. state that's home to significant landmarks from the American Civil Rights Movement. It was originally home to many Native Americans tribes including (Cherokee, Choctaw, Chikasaw, and Creek). After several wars between the Spanish, French, and British, it was recognized as a state in December 1819.The state has a population of 5.04 million as of 2021. The state capital is Montgomery and the largest city is Huntsville. The state is known as Alabama the beautiful because of the southern hospitality and nature scenes.


Place to visit: Marshall Space Center

NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center is situated on the U.S. Army's Redstone Arsenal in Huntsville, Alabama.Tour the nearby U.S. Space and Rocket Center, the world’s largest spaceflight museum—and home to Space Camp.
